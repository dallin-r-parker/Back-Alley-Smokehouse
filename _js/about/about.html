Best damn brisket you ever got from a nail salon

When the economy tanked, a down on his luck ex-carpenter who lost everything sold his car and used the money to start a smokehouse in the back room of a salon.

On his opening day, the street in front of his restaurant closed for construction. Jon's first customers were the construction workers blocking his entrance from the public.

From that one room, due to the quality of his food, Jon expanded to a small seating area and finally into the entire building.

He eventually married the owner of the beauty parlor, and today the two of them run the smokehouse together.
